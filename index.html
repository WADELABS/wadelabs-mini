Rebuilding Lives Through Computer Literacy

Wadelabs is an AI-guided computer learning environment that teaches true beginners how to use the tools they already have—safely, confidently, and independently.

Join Early Access Partner With Us

What Problem Are We Solving?

Millions of adults—especially people from low-income, rural, and justice-impacted backgrounds—never received real computer education. They’re handed phones, laptops, and apps, but no one explains how any of it works. That gap keeps them out of jobs, training, and opportunity.

Our Solution

Wadelabs adds a coaching layer on top of a familiar computer environment. As users click, type, and explore, an AI guide explains what’s happening, suggests next steps, and turns each action into a mini lesson. Instead of lectures, people learn by doing—inside a safe, structured digital space.

Who Wadelabs Is For

  • Adults who never had formal computer training
  • People returning from prison or long gaps in the workforce
  • Low-income and rural learners without tech mentors
  • Community colleges, re-entry programs, and nonprofits that need a simple, guided way to teach digital skills

Long-Term Vision

Wadelabs will grow into a platform that institutions can deploy in labs, re-entry programs, and community centers to deliver consistent, high-quality digital literacy training—powered by AI, grounded in real life.
